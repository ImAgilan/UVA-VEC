TechStart Global Rebrand

6 Week Project
Branding & Web
2023

Complete brand overhaul for international tech startup including visual identity, messaging, and digital presence

The Challenge

TechStart, a rapidly growing SaaS company, found their original branding no longer reflected their market position or ambitious vision. Their visual identity was inconsistent across platforms, their messaging failed to communicate their unique value proposition, and their digital presence couldn't scale with their expanding global operations.

The company needed a complete brand transformation that would:

  • Establish them as leaders in their space
  • Resonate with enterprise clients while remaining approachable
  • Work across diverse cultural markets
  • Provide clear guidelines for consistent implementation

Our Approach

We began with an intensive discovery phase, interviewing stakeholders, analyzing competitors, and auditing their existing brand assets. Our research revealed key insights that informed our strategy:

We developed a new brand architecture that balanced professionalism with innovation. The visual identity system incorporated:

  • A dynamic color palette that worked in both digital and print
  • A custom typeface with tech-inspired details
  • An abstract geometric mark representing connection and growth
  • Photography guidelines emphasizing human-centric technology
