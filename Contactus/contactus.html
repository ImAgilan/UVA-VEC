Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about working with us

What types of projects does UVA VEC take on?

We specialize in digital transformation projects including brand strategy, web and mobile development, UX/UI design, and digital marketing. Our team works with clients across industries, from startups to enterprises, helping them create impactful digital experiences.

How do I get started with a project?

The process begins with a discovery call where we learn about your goals, challenges, and requirements. From there, we'll provide a proposal outlining our recommended approach, timeline, and investment. Once approved, we assemble the perfect team and begin your project.

What are your typical response times?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our main office line and we'll connect you with the appropriate team member immediately.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! With offices in Sri Lanka and the UK, plus remote team members across time zones, we're equipped to serve clients worldwide. Over 60% of our clients are based outside our home countries.

What makes UVA VEC different from other agencies?

We combine global expertise with local insights, delivering world-class digital solutions at competitive rates. Our strategic approach ensures every project drives measurable business results, not just aesthetic improvements. Plus, our transparent process and dedicated teams create a partnership experience rather than a vendor relationship.
